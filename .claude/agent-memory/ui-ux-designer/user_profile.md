Jerónimo Álvarez is the founder and CEO of an AI automation agency based in Medellín, Colombia.
He acts as Product Manager and final design validator — no design goes to the Developer without his sign-off.
He communicates in Spanish. His clients are Colombian SMBs (joyerías, field sales teams, CEOs).
He has a strong product instinct and expects the designer to flag UX risks proactively, not just execute requests.
